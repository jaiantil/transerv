The Intelligence

AI doesn't just schedule.
It orchestrates.

Three layers of intelligence transform scattered requests into perfectly clustered, optimized, city-wide efficiency.

01
Ingestion
Requests arrive and are mapped in real time
As customers book services, every request is geo-tagged, timestamped, and classified. The system builds a live demand map — pinpointing exactly where services are needed and when.
02
Clustering
Requests are grouped by proximity and time window
Our AI clusters adjacent requests into logical routes — respecting appointment windows, service types, and technician specializations. Each cluster becomes a dense, efficient work block.
03
Dispatch
The right technician gets the perfect route
Matched by skill, proximity, and availability — every technician receives a pre-optimized daily route. No wasted travel, no dead time, no guesswork. Just clean, profitable work.
